Our Mission

Project A.W.A.R.E. (Amoeba Waterborne Alert & Risk Evaluation) was developed for the NASA Hackathon to address the rare but critical threat of *Naegleria fowleri*. Our mission is to combine public health education with a forward-thinking technological solution that uses NASA's Earth observation data to create a real-time, predictive water safety system.
