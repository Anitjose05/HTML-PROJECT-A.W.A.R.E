Stay Safe with Automatic Alerts

Our vision includes a companion mobile app that uses your phone's location to provide proactive, life-saving alerts if you unknowingly travel near a high-risk hotspot.

1. Enable Geo-Alerts

In the app, you would grant location permissions and enable alerts. This allows the app to monitor your location safely in the background.

2. Real-Time Monitoring

The app continuously cross-references your location with our live database of satellite-identified risk zones (geofencing).

3. Instant Push Notifications

If you enter a pre-defined risk zone, the app immediately sends a push notification to your device, warning you before you even get near the water.
